The story of the week

Sliprain sweeps four—but the final word belongs to the field

Sliprain put together a formidable midweek run, taking four daily wins across Brands Hatch, the Red Bull Ring, Hidden Valley and Monza. Then Uncle Glock produced the outright lap of the week: a razor-sharp 0:45.053 around Vallelunga Club.

Dominic6767 closed the week by conquering the Nordschleife in 6:35.356, a fitting finish after seven wildly different challenges. But the headline is bigger than any single result: 38 drivers showed up, chased personal bests and kept the timing screen alive all week.
