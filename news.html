20 July 2026 • Server news

Three manufacturers. One weekly showdown.

The new Cobber Racing GT3 Manufacturer Showdown puts BMW, McLaren and Mercedes head-to-head in a week-long hotlap fight—with every clean lap helping to build your manufacturer's result.

GT3 Manufacturer Showdown
Read moreHow to join, represent a manufacturer and follow the results

How it works

Choose your car when you join the server

Join the server and choose the BMW, McLaren or Mercedes you want to represent. Set your fastest clean laps in that car and help your manufacturer climb the weekly standings.

The server cannot prevent drivers from changing cars, so the manufacturer battle runs on the honour system. You can test the field, but if you want to fight for a team, choose your manufacturer and represent it properly for the weekly round.

A new circuit goes live each week, giving everyone seven days to learn the track, find the final few tenths and improve their position. The cars are official Kunos content, so no additional car mods are required.

Weekly results and manufacturer standings are published every Monday on the Cobber Racing website.
